# Brand Source Summary

Source: `Aluye Naturals Our Story.pdf` and supplied product photography.

## Core story

Aluye Naturals connects modern self-care with natural beauty rituals passed through generations. Its signature material is 100% unrefined shea butter sourced from West Africa, supported by ingredients such as African black soap, charcoal, coconut, grapeseed, olive, rosehip, jojoba, black seed, tea tree, and rosemary.

## Product families found in the source

- Scented and raw whipped body butter
- Face and body scrub
- Rosehip face and body oil
- Activated charcoal cleanser
- Organic African black soap
- Coconut and black seed oils
- Beard balm, beard wash, comb, and brush
- Clay mask
- Hair and scalp serum
- Loofah pads
- Lip balm
- Shea-based sun-care concept

## Existing visual cues

- Black packaging
- Kraft/tan labels
- Black serif typography
- Handmade, ingredient-forward character
- Ankh-like brand symbol

These cues inform the warm black, bone, kraft, gold, clay, and botanical palette in `MASTER.md`.
