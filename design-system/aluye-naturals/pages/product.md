# Product Detail Override

Use `../MASTER.md` unless overridden here.

## Goal

Answer “Is this right for me?” and make adding the correct variant effortless.

## Desktop layout

- Left: image gallery, 58–62% width.
- Right: sticky purchase panel, 38–42% width.

## Purchase panel order

1. Category/ritual eyebrow.
2. Product title.
3. Rating and review count.
4. One-sentence benefit.
5. Price and size.
6. Scent/variant selection.
7. Stock/delivery information.
8. Quantity and Add to Bag.
9. Wishlist.
10. Trust notes: raw ingredients, ethical sourcing, secure checkout.

## Detail content

- What it does
- Best for
- Key ingredients and origins
- Texture and scent
- How to use / ritual
- Full ingredients
- Shipping and returns
- Reviews
- Complete the ritual recommendations

Do not bury ingredients or usage inside inaccessible hover content. On mobile, use a sticky bottom Add to Bag bar after the main CTA scrolls away.
