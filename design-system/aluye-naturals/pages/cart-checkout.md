## Cart

- Show delivery threshold/progress without manufacturing urgency.
- Make quantity, variant, remove, and subtotal easy to edit.
- Cross-sell no more than three genuinely complementary products.
- Preserve cart server-side for signed-in users and safely in session for guests.

## Checkout

- Allow guest checkout.
- Use a clear sequence: Contact → Delivery → Payment → Review.
- Keep order summary visible on desktop and collapsible on mobile.
- Use address autocomplete only as enhancement; manual entry remains available.
- Display total costs before payment, including delivery and tax.
- Never request account creation before purchase; offer it after confirmation.
- Focus the first invalid field and provide an error summary.

## Confirmation
