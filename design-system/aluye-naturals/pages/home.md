# Home Page Override

Use `../MASTER.md` unless overridden here.

## Goal

Introduce the brand in seconds, establish product trust, and move visitors into category or best-seller shopping.

## Section order

1. Utility offer/delivery bar.
2. Header and category navigation.
3. Editorial hero: product/skin ritual image, concise benefit-led headline, Shop Best Sellers CTA, Discover Our Story secondary link.
4. Shop by Ritual: Body, Face, Hair & Scalp, Beard, Raw Essentials.
5. Best Sellers product rail/grid with ratings and quick add.
6. Shea provenance split section: West African sourcing, unrefined quality, artisan relationship.
7. Shop by Concern.
8. Ingredient spotlight cards.
9. “Body. Mind. Soul.” ritual/editorial feature.
10. Reviews/social proof.
11. Journal/education cards.
12. Newsletter and full footer.

## Rules

- Hero headline: maximum 10 words; supporting copy maximum 28 words.
- Keep the first product module visible within roughly two desktop viewports.
- Use product photography for conversion and editorial photography for story.
- Promotional modules should feel curated, not like a discount marketplace.
- Mobile product rails may horizontally scroll with a visible next-card cue.
