# Collection and Search Override

Use `../MASTER.md` unless overridden here.

## Goal

Make a broad catalogue easy to scan and narrow by ritual, concern, ingredient, form, scent, and price.

## Structure

1. Breadcrumbs.
2. H1, concise collection description, and result count.
3. Optional editorial banner beneath the description.
4. Filter/sort toolbar.
5. Product grid.
6. Pagination or Load More; do not use endless scroll without a footer escape.
7. SEO/education copy below the catalogue.

## Filters

- Category
- Concern
- Ingredient
- Product type
- Scent / unscented
- Skin or hair type
- Price
- Availability

Keep filters in the URL so pages are shareable and back navigation restores state.
