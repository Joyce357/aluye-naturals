Our story

Nature’s finest, carried forward.

Aluyè Naturals brings time-tested West African ingredients into modern skin, hair, body and grooming rituals.

Body. Mind. Soul.

Care with roots.

Our journey began with a simple belief: nature already provides remarkable materials for caring for skin and hair. The role of Aluyè is to select them thoughtfully, preserve their character and make them easy to welcome into everyday life.

At the heart of the range is unrefined shea butter sourced from West Africa. Its natural richness supports our body butters, beard care and hair treatments, while African black soap, charcoal and cold-pressed oils broaden the ritual.

We believe self-care should feel effective, grounded and generous—not complicated. Every product is an invitation to slow down and reconnect with the body.
